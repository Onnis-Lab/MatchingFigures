Welcome to the Figure Matching Game!

In this game, you and another player will each receive a set of 6 irregular figures. The figures are the same for both players, but the order is different.

Your goal is to communicate with the other player to figure out the correct matching order of the figures. As the main player, you will enter the 6 indices of the other player's figures that match the figures on your screen.
